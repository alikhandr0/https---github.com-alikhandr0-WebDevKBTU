Sushi Recipe

  • Time: 45 minuts
  • Serves: 4 rolls
Ingredients Quantity
Sushi Rice 2 cups
Water 2 cups
Rice Vinegar 2 cups
Salt 1 tsp
Nori Sheets 4 sheets
Cucumber 1/2 medium
Sushi Rice 2 cups

Steps:

  1. Cook the sushi rice and season with a mixture of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo rolling mat. Spread a layer of sushi rice.
  3. Layer with imitation crab, avocado, and cucumber.
  4. Roll tightly using the mat. Slice into rolls and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 30 minuts
  • Serves: 4 piece
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 250 g
Water 150ml
Dry Yeast 10 g
Salt 1 tsp
Nori Sheets 4 sheets
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Tomato Paste 150 g
Mozarella Cheese 150 g
Fresh Basil A few leaves

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough: combine flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil. Knead and let it rise.
  2. Roll out the dough into a circle.
  3. Spread tomato paste on the dough, top with mozzarella and basil leaves.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until done.

Cake

  • Time: 40 minuts
  • Serves: 8 piece
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200 g
Sugar 200 g
Eggs 2 pcs
Vegetable Oil 100 ml
Cocoa Powder 50 g
Milk 200 ml
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder).
  2. Add the wet ingredients (eggs, oil, milk, vanilla) and mix well.
  3. Pour the batter into a pan and bake in a preheated oven until done.
